Advantour


Overview

Our development team, Leaders In Technology (LIT), created an Android application that is the perfect companion to any user who wishes to explore nearby locations or take a tour around an unfamiliar place. Adventour can be used in a variety of situations: whether you would like to share a tour of an area you have just visited, or you are bored and looking for a new place to explore, this application can be used to document your adventures and keep them as memories to revisit. When going to a new city or visiting a college campus, you can view tours created by others and receive suggestions of what landmarks or “checkpoints” you should visit. The user can share a new tour they have created to give others the opportunity to experience the same adventure. One of the most popular use cases for this application is also to follow hikes or trails created by others. It is also useful to create a “tour” so you can find your way back to your starting location.

The main features of the application are Creating an Account, Logging In, Browsing Tours, Taking Tours, Creating Tours, and Viewing Tours. After creating a new account and logging in, the user has the opportunity to browse tours nearby or around any specified location. The tours can be viewed in a map view or a list view. After selecting an interesting tour, the user can navigate to the start and begin taking the tour. As the user takes the tour, they will be notified as they approach checkpoints and can view the images and notes left by the creator of the tour. When creating your own tour, the user can choose a beginning location and enter an overview or summary of the tour. The tours that a user has created and the tours that they have completed can be viewed in scrollable list.
